AQUA SYNC Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 25, 2025

AQUA SYNC is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data, including names, emails, or browsing history. The extension uses chrome.storage.local to save non-sensitive settings (e.g., reminder intervals, enabled methods like push/flash/rain, and restricted sites) locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Permissions

AQUA SYNC uses the following permissions:

  • activeTab: Enables optional Flash (50 sparkles) or Rain (100 drops) animations on the active tab, triggered by user action. No webpage content is accessed.
  • storage: Saves user preferences locally for functionality.
  • alarms: Schedules reminders at user-selected intervals (5-60 mins).
  • notifications: Displays push notifications with hydration tips.
  • scripting: Injects scripts for animations, with no content alteration.

Restricted Sites

Flash and Rain animations are disabled by default on sites containing keywords like 'mail', 'bank', 'zoom.com', 'teams', 'meet.google.com', 'slack', 'webex', and others (e.g., paypal.com, discord.com). Users can add custom restrictions in settings.

Accessibility

Flash animations may affect photosensitive users. Disable them via settings for a safer experience.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, transfer, or use your data for purposes unrelated to reminders or creditworthiness.
